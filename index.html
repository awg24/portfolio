About me

I went through a 3-month full-time coding bootcamp called The Iron Yard back in May 2015 where I learned primarily front-end development. Since then, I've picked up a few remote jobs/contracts where I really honed my skills as a developer.

Through the course of my most recent job, I found that I get a real kick out of making APIs. For whatever reason, I have a lot of fun using Postman to hit endpoints I've made and seeing the response pop up (or a 500 error that I have to debug). Aside from APIs, I really like building out new tools, or features, for clients. There's just something that's extremely gratifying about turning a thought, or in most cases a need, and making it into a reality.
